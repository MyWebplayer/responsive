About Webplayer

This website is a personalized webplayer that is intended to play music on the background while doing daily tasks and could still work even if there is no internet available. This webplayer is an open source where user can freely add,remove, and modify music to a library.

Sources

sources gathered in this website usually came from the main sources of music or songs such as spotify and youtube. while sources to download and/or convert song files are on a free web called spotiflyer by github. This website also refers to billboard 100, spotify, and youtube recommended videos/songs as reference for most played, top hits, hot picks and most recommended songs up to date.

Forum

Forum page is a threaded page where you can find a thread intended solely for updates and patches. While there is a different thread for group discussions and a question and answer forum. Any recommendations and suggestions will also be included on this tab/page on a separate thread.

Services

Services offered by this website are to give costumers/consumers/users the ability to design/costumize/modify their own webplayer design while they can have the option to request an editor/developer to design for them (however charges may or may not apply)
